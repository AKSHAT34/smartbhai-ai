## 💡 Posting Tips

1. **Time it right**: Post Tue-Thu, 8:30-10am IST or 5-7pm IST (Indian tech audience peaks)
2. **First 90 min matter**: Reply to every single comment in the first hour. LinkedIn ranks engagement velocity.
3. **Hook test**: If line 1 doesn't stop the scroll, rework it. Lead with a number or a contrarian take.
4. **Add a screenshot**: Carousel posts with a phone mockup of the app get 3-5x reach. Screenshot the Chat + Options screens.
5. **Tag strategically**: Tag 2-3 creators in Indian fintech/PM space in the comments, not the post (avoids spam flag).
6. **Don't paste all hashtags inline** — put 10-15 in the post body and the rest in the FIRST COMMENT. LinkedIn's algorithm treats that comment as additional discovery surface.
7. **Pin the GitHub link comment** to the top.
8. **Cross-post**: X/Twitter with shortened text, Reddit r/IndianStreetBets + r/IndiaInvestments (adjust tone — no hashtags), Hacker News "Show HN".
